Best Pickled Jalepenos Recipe

This is the only pickled jalepeno recipe you will ever need! Pickled

Pickled jalepenos are something that are best left simple. Too many ingredients will wash out the flavor of the pepper itself and take away from the essence of the jalepnos. Three simple ingredients scaled in the appropriate ratio will leave you with a delectable vinegary pickled jalepeno to add to a sandwich, salad, or anything else you want to spice up with a little twang.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups jalepenos, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Put sliced jalepenos into sealable jar
  2. Pour 2 cups vinegar into a pot and bring to a boil
  3. Add sugar and salt to vinegar and stir until completely dissolved
  4. Pour boiling vinegar into jar and seal immediately
  5. Let cool on the counter then referigerate for minimum 48 hours before eating
